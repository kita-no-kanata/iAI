# Product Principles

These principles should guide every design and development decision.

---

## 1. Structure over Prices

Users should understand economic structures before looking at stock prices.

The product visualizes relationships, not numbers.

---

## 2. Discovery over Searching

Users should naturally discover new industries, companies, and trends.

The experience should feel like exploring Google Maps.

---

## 3. Industries before Companies

Companies are only one component of an industry.

The industry should always provide the first layer of understanding.

---

## 4. AI should improve understanding

AI should never simply recommend buying or selling.

Instead, AI should explain why something matters.

---

## 5. Connections create value

The most valuable information is the connection between events.

Example

AI Boom

↓

GPU

↓

Semiconductor

↓

Data Center

↓

Electricity

↓

Telecommunication

---

## 6. Long-term thinking

The product should encourage understanding of long-term trends rather than short-term speculation.

---

## 7. Minimal but Information Rich

Professional users appreciate information density.

However, every piece of information should have a clear purpose.

---

## 8. Every screen answers one question

Every page should answer a single question.

Home
"What is happening in the world?"

Industry
"What is happening in this industry?"

Company
"What is happening in this company?"

Theme
"How does this trend spread?"
